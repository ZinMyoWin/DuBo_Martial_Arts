About Us

Welcome to DoBu Martial Arts - a gym like no other. Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive environment for people of all ages and abilities to learn and practice martial arts, improve their fitness, and gain self-confidence and discipline.

Meet Our Instructors

Harpreet Kaur

Harpreet Kaur is a highly qualified fitness coach with a BSc in Physiotherapy and an MSc in Sports Science. With a strong background in both fields, Harpreet is able to create fitness programs that are not only effective but also safe and tailored to each individual's needs. She has a particular interest in injury prevention and rehabilitation, and uses her expertise to help clients reach their full potential.

Morris Davis

Morris Davis is a highly skilled assistant martial arts coach who brings a wealth of experience to our team. With a 3rd Dan Blackbelt in karate and accreditation as a Muay Thai coach, he has developed a deep understanding of multiple martial arts disciplines. Morris is committed to helping students develop their skills and achieve their goals, and his expertise and enthusiasm make him a valuable member of our coaching staff.

Traci Santiago

Traci Santiago is a highly qualified fitness coach with a BSc in Sports Science and certification in health and nutrition. She specializes in devising strength and conditioning programs tailored specifically for combat athletes, helping them to improve their overall fitness and performance. With her expertise and knowledge, Traci is dedicated to helping her clients achieve their fitness goals and reach their full potential.

Guy Victory

Guy Victory is an assistant martial arts coach with expertise in Brazilian jiu-jitsu and judo. He holds a 2nd Dan Blackbelt in jiu-jitsu and a 1st Dan Blackbelt in judo. With his experience and skills, Guy is committed to helping students improve their techniques and reach their full potential. He is an integral part of the coaching team, and his dedication and passion for martial arts inspire others to strive for excellence.

Sarah Nova

Sarah Nova is an assistant martial arts coach at DoBu Martial Arts gym. She has an impressive 5th Dan black belt in karate and is highly skilled in teaching karate techniques to students of all levels. With her vast experience and knowledge in the field, Sarah is a valuable asset to the martial arts coaching team at DoBu.

Mauricio Gomez

Mauricio Gomez is the owner and head martial arts coach of DoBu Martial Arts. He is a highly skilled and experienced coach who is proficient in all martial arts. He holds a 4th Dan Blackbelt in judo, a 3rd Dan Blackbelt in jiu-jitsu, and a 1st Dan Blackbelt in karate. Additionally, he is an accredited Muay Thai coach.
